CAPTAIN AMERICA

Real Name: Steven "Steve" Rogers

Height: 6'2"

Weight: 220 lbs

Powers: Captain America represented the pinnacle of human physical perfection. He experienced a time when he was augmented to superhuman levels, but generally performed just below superhuman levels for most of his career.

Abilities: Captain America had mastered the martial arts of American-style boxing and judo, and had combined these disciplines with his own unique hand-to-hand style of combat.
