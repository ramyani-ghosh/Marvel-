ABOUT US

We are a bunch of marvel enthusiats who would like to spend all their time watching Marvel movies and reading about them. And don't we all love buying merchandise? This project was an attempt at bringing information and utilities relating to the heroes we all know and love by providing a simple user interface and designing a market ready website. We have a store that allows user to buy Marvel merchandise and it has a wiki page that lets the user access the details of a few selected heroes.
